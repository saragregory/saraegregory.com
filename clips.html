Investigative reporting

With a team from The Columbus Dispatch, I reported a series of stories about campus crime that showed how colleges frequently impose light punishments for violent crime. "Campus Insecurity" was published in fall 2014 and was supported in part with a grant from the Fund for Investigative Journalism.
